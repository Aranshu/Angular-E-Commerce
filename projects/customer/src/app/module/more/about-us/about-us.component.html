About us

From an early age, JITAN KAUR knew she wanted her own fashion brand. Established in 2020. Her designs are a unique blend of contemporary and minimalistic and wants to bring individualistic design elements in everyday wear. JITAN KAUR offers ready to wear ethic, western, diffusion clothing. All lines are manufactured in house using ETHICAL TRADE PRACTICES, at JITAN KAUR.
